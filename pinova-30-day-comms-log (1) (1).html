Rules & Reference

Every real conversation

  1. Pause 2 seconds before answering.
  2. One idea per sentence.
  3. Every sentence answers: what happened / why it matters / what's next.
  4. Split any sentence over 12 words into two.

Trust sequencing (from Day 22)

Before any sales or investor call, write one line: "What does this person need to hear first to trust me, before they hear the rest?" Open with that line. Say the honest weak points after, not before.

Weekly focus

  • Days 1–7 — Clarity: speak slowly, cut filler words.
  • Days 8–14 — Structure: Problem → Reason → Solution.
  • Days 15–21 — Trust: questions and listening over talking.
  • Days 22–30 — Persuasion: objections, sales, negotiation.
